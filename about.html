About BUSTANI SMARTHOMEZ

Our Story

Founded in 2024, BUSTANI SMARTHOMEZ began as a small family-run guesthouse with just 5 rooms. What started as a passion project quickly grew into one of the most beloved accommodations in the city, thanks to our commitment to exceptional service and attention to detail.

Today, we offer 25 beautifully appointed rooms and suites, each designed to provide the perfect blend of comfort and luxury. Our team of dedicated professionals works tirelessly to ensure every guest feels at home from the moment they arrive.

Our Mission

At BUSTANI SMARTHOMEZ, our mission is simple: to create unforgettable experiences for our guests by providing exceptional hospitality in a warm, welcoming environment. We believe that travel should be comfortable, inspiring, and stress-free.

We source locally whenever possible, from our organic breakfast ingredients to the artwork decorating our walls, supporting our community while offering guests an authentic taste of our region.
